Nobodys of NYC — Cloak

Cloak

Cloak lets you know where all your friends, "friends," and nonfriends are at all times so you never have to run into that special someone. Think of it as the antisocial network.

usecloak.com

Cloak uses your Instagram account and your Foursquare account to remain aware of your "friends" last known locations.

Utilizing background location, background fetch, and location notifications, Cloak requires minimal attention. Just set it and forget it and Cloak will alert you when you get too close to anyone you'd rather avoid.
